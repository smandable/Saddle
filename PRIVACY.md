# Privacy Policy — Saddle Drive Manager

**Last updated:** March 17, 2026

## Overview

Saddle Drive Manager ("Saddle") is a macOS menu bar utility for managing external drives. Your privacy is important to us, and Saddle is designed with privacy in mind.

## Data Collection

Saddle does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking** — Saddle does not include any analytics frameworks or tracking code.
- **No network connections** — Saddle does not connect to the internet for any reason.
- **No personal information** — Saddle does not access or store any personal information.

## Local Data

Saddle stores a single configuration file on your Mac containing your preferences (drive groups, aliases, and settings). This file is stored locally in your Application Support directory and is never transmitted anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

Saddle does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/smandable/Saddle](https://github.com/smandable/Saddle/issues).
